This weekend, 186 people took part in this weekend's Pocket parkrun including 33 first timers and 21 people who achieved new PBs. [Owen Edwards](https://www.parkrun.org.uk/pocket/results/latestresults/athletehistory?athleteNumber=2378248)[![50_club_mini](https://images.parkrun.com/blogs.dir/1667/files/2019/02/50_club_mini-e1550336989477.jpg)](https://images.parkrun.com/blogs.dir/1667/files/2019/02/50_club_mini-e1550336989477.jpg) was the first finisher this week with a time of 19:15 and [Natalie Jane Morgan](https://www.parkrun.org.uk/pocket/results/latestresults/athletehistory?athleteNumber=54524) was the first female finisher in 21:32. [Meriel Howe](https://www.parkrun.org.uk/pocket/results/latestresults/athletehistory?athleteNumber=525409) had the highest age grade with 78.82% and a new PB of 30:13 **New Club Members**
